--- Steps to building the Best PB&J EVER!!! ---

  1. one: Get Bread, PB and J

  2. two: Place two slices side by side on clean surface

  3. three: Spread PB on one slice to 1/4 inch thick

  4. four: Spread butter on both slices 0.1 inch thick

  5. five: Spread Jam on slice with only butter on it to 1/4 inch thick

  6. six: Fold slices together with PB & J facing together

  7. seven: ENJOY!
